About

     Hirokazu Tanaka may not be a well known artist in the west, but I would consider him to be the best video game sountrack composer. While he's done work on japanese movies and other media, my personal favourite soundtrack he's done is the soundtrack for Earthbound, which also happens to be my favourite game. The reason I say he's the best video game soundtrack composer is because no one else can really complement the game like he did. His music really makes Earthbound, Earthbound.
