About

     Daft Punk is a famous parisian electronic duo that has produced music for 28 years. Their tracks have always stayed relevant to me, and I've grown up listening to their music my entire life. Coincedentally, the day I began working on this webpage, Daft Punk split up. Consider this page a tribute to the music they've given me and many others throughout the years.
